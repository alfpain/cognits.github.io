What's a dedicated team?

Its a full functioning software developing team, that works just for you and your projects. Depending on the project that might mean 2 backend devs, 1 frontend dev, 1 QA dev, 1 UX designer and 1 Scrum Master.


How does your pricing work?

You pay per sprint a developer is active in your project. Prices vary between categories of developers. (ej. backend vs frontend developer). To learn more, call us at or we can schedule a Skype call this week.
